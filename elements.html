a bit about who I am

Since I was young, I have been constantly driven by the desire to understand how the world works. I'm proud to say that I've come a long way from electrocuting myself while taking apart an old desktop at twelve, but that curiosity never ceased. Through a bachelors degree in Biology I discovered just how much of the world could be interpreted through data. I decided I wanted to pursue a career which would allow me to explore and visualize these data to reveal new ideas and solutions to the questions around me.

My name is Gareth Bennett, a data analyst, environmentalist, puzzle solver, and strategy game enthusiast who enjoys finding answers and explanations for why things happen. My continuous desire to learn and discover is what motivates me to move forward in this space. I learn best through explaining complex topics to others through clean data visualization and story telling.
